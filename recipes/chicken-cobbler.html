Instructions

Melt a stick of butter in a casserole dish, then add the chicken and veggies on top of that. Add seasoning.

Mix the milk and biscuit mix in a big measuring cup, then pour over the chicken and veggies.

Mix the broth and cream of mushroom soup in the same big measuring cup, then pour it over everything else.

Bake at 350 degrees for about an hour. And don't be afraid to mix up the soup, seasoning, & veggie choices!
